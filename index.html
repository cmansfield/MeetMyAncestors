Welcome to Meet My Ancestors

The game requires you to login to your Family Search account. When the login button on the next page is pressed, it will take you to your Family Search login. Once logged in, you will be automatically directed to the game.

A New Way to Explore Your Family Tree

Play Meet My Ancestors!
The game will be web based and open in a new tab.
Create a game profile and avatar.
The game will download your family tree.
You can visit with the other characters and visit the home. Press the space bar to interact.
